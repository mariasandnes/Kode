PUBLIC TRANSPORT

Best way to get around Train - metro - tram Remember to tap in and out. 24h tickets available
Airport → Amsterdam Central Direct train from Amsterdam airport Schiphold Remember to buy tickets before entering the train, you can find ticket machines in the station
Amsterdamn Central → Venue Metro line 54 to Gein or Train to Rotterdam Centraal You want to exit both the train and the metro at Amsterdam Bijlmer ArenA. Remember to tap out before leaving the station
Uber and taxi Both uber and taxies are available in Amsterdam Uber and taxis are more expensive and might take longer depending on the route
Walking Walking is a great option in Amsterdam, especially in the city centre Watch out for bikes and cars when walking and be careful walking alone at night
